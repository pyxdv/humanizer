## About the skill

The humanizer skill (v2.1.1) removes signs of AI-generated writing from text, making it sound more natural and human. It's based on Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing" guide and detects/fixes 24 common AI writing patterns including:

- Significance inflation
- Promotional language  
- Vague attributions
- AI vocabulary overuse
- Em dash overuse
- Rule of three patterns
- And 18 more patterns

For more details, see the README.md file.
